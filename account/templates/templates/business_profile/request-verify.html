Request to verify

To publish your Business Profile, we need to make sure you represent this business. To do that, we'll need some verification.

Documents you'll need

To verify your Business Profile we'll need a copy of your company's:

  • VAT certificate
  • Indemnity insurance
  • Bank, building society or credit card statement. The statement must be no more than three months old and show your name and current address.
