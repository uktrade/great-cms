Why we want to export

What’s the reason you want to export?
Every business will have its own motivations.
Common are:

  • grow the business by selling overseas
  • maintain growth when sales have levelled off in the UK
  • Spread risk by selling countries other than the UK
  • Take advantage of an increase in customers wanting your kind of product

It doesn’t matter if you’re reacting to an export opportunity or proactively planning, spelling them out will help make the most of either situation.
