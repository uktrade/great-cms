Why you want to export

What are the main reasons you want to export?

Common reasons for exporting include:

  • grow the business by selling overseas
  • maintain growth when sales in the UK have levelled off
  • spread risk evenly by selling in countries other than the UK
  • widen your customer base

Planning and thinking about your export objectives will help you make the most out of any export opportunities you are interested in.

What you want to achieve

What do you want to accomplish and by when?

To increase the prospect of success, ensure what you want to achieve is specific, measurable and realistic:

  • specific - your objective is well defined
  • measurable - use things like quantity, cost and time frame so you can judge how well you're doing
  • realistic - ambition is good but make sure your objective is within reach
